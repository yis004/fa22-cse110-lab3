Project 1 communication

Meeting description: Brainstorming regarding how to collaborate together in the upcoming quarter

Date - 10/4/2022

Attendance List: Alex Song, Juming Chao, Laoting Xu, Bob Moster



Meeting Agenda

We will start the meeting by discussing what collaboration looks like. This will help everyone be on the same page and understand what is expected of them.

Next, set some expectations for the meeting. For example, each person should have a chance to share their ideas before discussion begins.

Finally, spend some time brainstorming ideas on how to collaborate together. Brainstorming can be done individually or in small groups. Once everyone has had a chance to share their ideas, it is time to start discussion and come up with a plan of action.



Miscellaneous comments / questions / concerns

  1. How do I start collaborating with others?
  2. How can I be more effective when collaborating?
  3. What are some best practices for collaborating?
  4. What are some ways to collaborate effectively?
  5. How can we overcome any communication barriers?
  6. What are some effective methods for managing and tracking collaborations?

    7. Any other questions arise can be asked during the meeting...
